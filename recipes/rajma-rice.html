Rajma Rice


Description

Rajma Rice is a classic North Indian comfort food that brings together tender red kidney beans cooked in a thick, flavorful gravy made with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and a blend of traditional Indian spices. The curry is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the spices to infuse deeply into the beans, creating a rich and hearty dish that is both satisfying and nutritious.

Served over a bed of steaming basmati rice, Rajma Rice is a wholesome, vegetarian meal loved across generations. Its comforting flavors and balanced nutrition make it a popular choice not just in Indian homes, but also among food lovers worldwide seeking a taste of authentic Indian cuisine.

Ingredients Required:-

For Rajma:-

  • 1 cup red kidney beans (rajma) | soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion | finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes | pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as needed

For Rice:-

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps to follow :-

  1. Cook the Kidney Beans: Pressure cook soaked rajma with 3 cups of water and a pinch of salt for about 15–20 minutes or until soft. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Masala: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.

  3. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Stir in pureed tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.

  4. Simmer the Curry: Add cooked rajma with some of its water. Simmer for 15–20 minutes on low heat. Finish with garam masala and chopped coriander.

  5. Cook the Rice: Rinse rice until the water runs clear. Boil with salt and water until fluffy.

  6. Serve: Plate hot rajma over rice and garnish with fresh coriander.
